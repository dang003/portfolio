beneath the mask.

My sign is Taurus. My spirit animal is a deer. And if I were a type of bean, I’d be Lima.



An inspirational poster on the wall of my 4th grade classroom that stuck with me: “Your character is who you are when no one is watching.” It’d be easier to define my personality with constellations or online quizzes, but the real test happens when there are no consequences. At all times, I try to be helpful, kind, and respectful. If I see a problem, I try to fix it. I excel in team environments, and I work hard to create great designs. I hold myself accountable to be the best I can be when no one is watching. I’m not perfect, and I will continue to grow and learn from my mistakes.



But, if you really want to know, my Hogwarts house is Hufflepuff.

birth of a wish.

While teaching English in South Korea, I learned that there are plenty of problems in education world-wide. With the skills that I have developed, I hope to solve problems for teachers and students in the classroom and beyond.



My goal is to design products that can raise the level of education everywhere. I believe that we can build a better way to educate and learn. Education is the key to a brighter future, and I want to be there when we open the door.
